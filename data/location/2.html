Church

Estimate Action Time : oo

This is easiest among any other places. Dress clean and go on Sunday morning

People at church are usually friendly. At start, approach a group and introduce yourself.

It doesn't matter you're Christian or not.

Try to get to know everyone before talking to your target.


What to talk about ?

Be nice, remember to smile !. Talk about today activities

Example

Soft approach

"I'm new here, mind showing me around ?"

"The story in bible is quite interesting, but I do not understand some part..."

Cool approach

"Hi, my name is ..... is good to see angel today (look and smile at here)"

"I think I'm a demon.. (Wait until she asks why or talk to you.)"

Advice

Respect people there, respect their religion. Being cool is not being cocky.

Find a church to start with, you can ask your friends around or via website around your area. Church is a good place to make friends after all.
